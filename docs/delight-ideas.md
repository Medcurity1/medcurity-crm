# Delight ideas — little joys that make Pulse fun

Brainstorm from Nathan's 2026-07-01 ask, in the spirit of the seasonal login
background everyone loves. Ranked by joy-per-effort. Nothing here is built;
pick what sounds fun.

## Ready-to-love (small builds, big smiles)

1. **Meddy's seasonal moods** — the Meddy bot icon in the sidebar gets a tiny
   Santa hat in December, sunglasses in July, a pumpkin in October. Same
   system as the login backgrounds, but it follows you around the app all day.
   *(Small)*

2. **Tiered closed-won celebrations** — confetti already fires on Closed Won;
   scale it: bigger deals, bigger burst. Cross a threshold ($10k+?) and the
   whole team gets a banner on Home: "🔔 Molly just closed $12,000 with
   Cascade Valley!" Selling feels like winning. *(Small)*

3. **Friday wins recap** — Friday afternoons, Home shows a cheerful strip:
   "This week: 4 deals won, $32k booked, 61 calls logged. Go team." One
   glance of pride before the weekend. *(Small)*

4. **Friendly loading lines** — replace bare "Loading…" with rotating lines:
   "Rounding up your deals…", "Feeding Meddy…", "Warming up the pipeline…"
   Tiny personality on every page. *(Small)*

5. **Client anniversaries** — the account page (and a Home card) quietly
   celebrates: "🎉 Cascade Valley has been a client for 5 years this week."
   Doubles as a genuinely useful touchpoint prompt for the renewals team.
   *(Small-Medium)*

## Hidden treasures (discovery joy)

6. **The 404 mini-game** — our "page not found" becomes a tiny playable game
   (a little Meddy that jumps over paperwork, Chrome-dinosaur style). Dead
   ends become the most fun page on the site. *(Medium)*

7. **Konami code easter egg** — type the classic ↑↑↓↓←→←→BA anywhere and
   Meddy moonwalks across the screen with retro confetti. Zero UI footprint;
   pure word-of-mouth magic when someone discovers it. *(Small)*

8. **Sound pack seasons** — the notification sounds get optional seasonal
   variants (sleigh bells in December). Off by default, one click in
   Settings. *(Small)*

## People-warmth (opt-in)

9. **Work anniversaries & birthdays** — opt-in in My Settings; on your day,
   your login gets personal confetti and Home says happy birthday. Team
   feels seen. *(Small)*

10. **High-five reactions** — when a teammate's closed-won banner appears,
    click 🖐 to send them a high five (they get a happy little
    notification). Lightweight camaraderie, no comment threads. *(Medium)*

## Recommended starter pack

**#1 Meddy's moods + #2 tiered celebrations + #7 Konami egg** — all small,
zero risk, and they cover the three flavors: ambient charm, shared wins, and
a hidden secret for people to find.
