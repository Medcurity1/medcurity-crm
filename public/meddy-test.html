What We Do

Medcurity is a HIPAA compliance and cybersecurity platform built for healthcare organizations. We help clinics, hospitals, and health systems complete their annual HIPAA Security Risk Analysis, run Network Vulnerability Assessments, manage vendors and Business Associate Agreements, and maintain policies and documentation.

  • Security Risk Analysis (SRA) — Guided, AI-powered, audit-ready compliance documentation
  • Network Vulnerability Assessment (NVA) — Scan your network for security weaknesses
  • Medcurity Academy — HIPAA training for employees and compliance officers
  • Vendor Management — Track BAAs and vendor compliance in one place
  • PolicyScan — AI-powered policy review that auto-fills SRA questions
